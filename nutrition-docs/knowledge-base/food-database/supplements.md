## Creatine

| Type | Serving | Notes |
|------|---------|-------|
| Creatine Monohydrate | 5g/day | Gold standard, most researched |
| Micronized Creatine | 5g/day | Finer powder, mixes better |
| Creatine HCL | 1.5-2g/day | More soluble, smaller dose |

**Loading** (optional): 20g/day for 5-7 days, then 5g/day maintenance
**No loading**: Just take 5g daily, saturates in ~3-4 weeks

**Timing**: Any time, consistency matters more than timing

**Benefits**:
- Increased strength and power
- Better high-intensity performance
- Faster recovery
- May support cognitive function

**Calories**: Essentially zero (not metabolized for energy)

## Recommended Supplement Stack

### Tier 1 - Almost Everyone Benefits
- **Vitamin D3**: 1,000-5,000 IU (based on levels)
- **Omega-3 Fish Oil**: 1-3g EPA+DHA
- **Magnesium**: 200-400 mg (glycinate or citrate)
- **Creatine**: 5g daily (if training)

### Tier 2 - Situational
- **Protein powder**: If struggling to hit targets
- **Vitamin K2**: If taking D3
- **Zinc**: If deficient or frequent illness
- **B12**: If vegan

### Tier 3 - Performance Optimization
- **Pre-workout ingredients**: Caffeine, citrulline, beta-alanine
- **Collagen**: Joint health, skin
- **Ashwagandha**: Stress, recovery

### Skip / Waste of Money
- BCAAs (if adequate protein)
- Testosterone boosters
- Fat burners (beyond caffeine)
- Most "proprietary blends"
