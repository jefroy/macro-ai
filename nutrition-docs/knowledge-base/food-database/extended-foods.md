## Quick Reference: Foods by Nutrient

### High Vitamin D
Salmon, sardines, mackerel, eggs, fortified milk, mushrooms (UV-exposed)

### High Magnesium
Pumpkin seeds, spinach, dark chocolate, almonds, avocado, black beans

### High Iron
Beef liver, beef, lentils, spinach, fortified cereals, dark chocolate

### High Zinc
Oysters, beef, pumpkin seeds, chicken, chickpeas

### High Potassium
Avocado, potato, sweet potato, banana, salmon, spinach

### High Omega-3
Mackerel, salmon, sardines, flaxseed, chia seeds, walnuts

### High Fiber
Chia seeds, lentils, black beans, avocado, raspberries, oats
