## Quick Meal Components

### High-Protein, Low-Calorie
- Chicken breast (165 cal / 31g protein)
- Egg whites (17 cal / 3.6g protein per white)
- Greek yogurt 0% (59 cal / 10g protein per 100g)
- Cottage cheese low-fat (72 cal / 11g protein per 100g)
- Tuna in water (116 cal / 26g protein per 100g)
- Shrimp (99 cal / 24g protein per 100g)

### High-Volume, Low-Calorie (Great for Satiety)
- Cucumber: 16 cal per 100g
- Spinach: 23 cal per 100g
- Zucchini: 17 cal per 100g
- Tomato: 18 cal per 100g
- Mushrooms: 22 cal per 100g
- Cauliflower: 25 cal per 100g

### Energy-Dense (For Bulking/Adding Calories)
- Olive oil: 119 cal per tbsp
- Peanut butter: 188 cal per 2 tbsp
- Nuts: 150-185 cal per 28g
- Avocado: 322 cal per medium
- Whole milk: 150 cal per cup
