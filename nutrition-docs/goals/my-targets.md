## Cut-Phase Food Priorities

### High-Volume, Low-Calorie (Satiety)
- Leafy greens (spinach, kale)
- Cruciferous vegetables (broccoli, cauliflower)
- Cucumber, celery, tomatoes
- Egg whites
- Chicken breast
- White fish (cod, tilapia)

### Protein-Dense (Hit 170g)
- Chicken breast: 31g/100g
- Greek yogurt 0%: 10g/100g
- Cottage cheese: 11g/100g
- Egg whites: 11g/100g
- Whey protein: 25g/scoop
- Shrimp: 24g/100g

### Smart Carbs (Training Days)
- White rice (around workouts)
- Oats (breakfast)
- Sweet potato
- Fruit (bananas, berries)

### Healthy Fats (Limited but Essential)
- Fish oil (supplement)
- Eggs (whole, limit 2-3/day)
- Avocado (small portions)
- Olive oil (measured)

## Foods to Limit During Cut

| Food | Reason | Alternative |
|------|--------|-------------|
| Nuts/nut butter | Calorie dense, easy to overeat | Measure strictly or avoid |
| Cheese | High calorie, high fat | Low-fat cottage cheese |
| Cooking oils | Hidden calories | Spray oil, measure carefully |
| Dried fruit | Concentrated sugar | Fresh fruit |
| Granola | Calorie bomb | Plain oats |
| Alcohol | Empty calories, impairs fat loss | Skip or rare |

---

## Cut-Phase Tips

1. **Weigh food** - Eyeballing underestimates calories
2. **Protein first** - Build meals around protein source
3. **Volume eating** - Fill up on vegetables
4. **Stay hydrated** - 3L+ water, helps satiety
5. **Sleep priority** - Poor sleep = muscle loss, hunger
6. **Caffeine strategically** - Morning/pre-workout only
7. **Refeed if needed** - Higher carb day weekly if energy tanks
